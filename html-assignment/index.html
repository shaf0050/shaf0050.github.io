About me

Hi, I am Nitheesh Shafi Kumar. You can call me by my first name (Nitheesh). I completed my bachelor's degree in Multimedia in the year 2018. I have been working in the media field for the past five years. I have also worked as a graphic designer in Bahrain for a year. I've also proved my worth in photography as I am also a freelance photographer. My main hobby is to explore new places and, I spend my leisure time shooting photographs.
